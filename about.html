Barbie Samantha Lauren


When she's not writing code or having dance parties with Barbies in their dreamhouses, Samantha Lauren likes to do many other things.

Soccer

This Barbie is a soccer girlie. She played for almost 10 years growing up and now plays on adult leagues for fun. Soccer taught her how to be tough and strong and helped her develop new skills.


Piano

Samantha Lauren has been playing the piano for 13 years and it makes her happy. Playing piano is an outlet that allows her to be creative, learn new technical skills, and explore the language of music. She also loves when she gets the chance to learn a song for someone she cares about and play it for them.


Sunny Morning Strolls

She loves to walk, especially with her sweet dog. Feeling the warm sun on her face and the quiet sounds of nature in the morning time makes her feel peaceful and contributes to her mental well-being.
